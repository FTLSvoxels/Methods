Black and white method (OP):

1. New image
2. Paste image
3. Resize image 650x700
4. Make image black and white by going to Adjustments
5. Use Make transparent plugin by going to effects then colors
6. Make 2 layers
7. Layer 2 goes under nsfw image
8. Layer 3 goes ontop the nsfw image
9. Use black paint in layer 2 & 3
10. Draw a bait like a house or car with paintbrush on layer 3 (COLOR OF PAINTBRUSH: WHITE)
11. Magic wand the white stuff in paintbrush (Quicker way to get all the white stuff from the image is shift+Click)
12. Go to the nsfw layer (background) and cut it and make a new layer and paste it
13. use 150 Opacity on Layer 4
14. Use 181 Opacity on Background
15. Use 25 Opacity on Layer 3
